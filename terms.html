Privacy Policy

We value your trust. Here is a quick summary of how TransformX Technologies handles your data:

  • Data Collection: We only collect what is necessary to provide our software products and services (e.g., name, contact info, and usage data).
  • Purpose Limitation: Your data is used strictly for fulfilling our service contracts, improving our products, and legal compliance. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Your Consent: We process your data only with your explicit consent. You have the right to withdraw this consent at any time.
  • Security Standards: We follow ISO 27001-aligned security practices and use industry-standard encryption to keep your data safe.
  • Data Rights: Under Indian Law (DPDPA), you have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data held by us.
  • Retention: We only keep your data as long as needed to provide our services or as required by Indian regulatory authorities (like CERT-In).

⚖️ Your Rights as a Data Principal

As a user of TransformX, you are empowered with the following rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA):

  • Right to Information: Know what data we have and who we share it with.
  • Right to Correction: Update any inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data once our service is complete.
  • Right to Grievance: Seek redressal if you feel your data is mishandled.
